How Can We Help?

Using InSitu

Create flashcards from words, review them with flip cards, and keep your vocabulary practice focused across Turkish, English, Dutch, and Russian.

AI Generation

When you generate cards, InSitu sends only the text needed for that request to the AI provider. Review generated content before relying on it for study.

Local Progress

Your cards, study stats, XP, and streaks are stored locally on your device. InSitu does not require an account or subscription.

FAQ

What should I include in a support request?

  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • The action you were trying to complete.
  • Any error message or screenshot that helps explain the issue.

Does InSitu collect analytics?

No. InSitu is designed as a personal study app without login, telemetry, or analytics tracking.

Can I use it offline?

Studying existing cards works locally. AI card generation requires an internet connection.

Privacy Summary

InSitu keeps your flashcards and study progress on your device. The app does not use user accounts, subscriptions, third-party analytics, or advertising. AI generation may send the words or text you provide to the configured AI service so cards can be created.

Need help with InSitu?

Open a GitHub issue for bugs, questions, or support requests. Turkish or English support messages are welcome.
